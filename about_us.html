Our History

SPAR started in the Netherlands in 1932 when a businessman named Adriaan van Well created a group for independent grocery store owners. His idea was to help small shops work together so they could compete with bigger businesses. The name SPAR comes from a Dutch phrase that means people benefit when they work together. SPAR later expanded to many countries around the world. In South Africa, SPAR was established in 1963, with its first stores opening in Durban. The business grew by allowing local store owners to operate under the trusted SPAR brand while still managing their own stores. Today, SPAR is one of the most recognised retail groups in South Africa. It operates supermarkets, liquor stores, pharmacies, and other retail outlets. Our company is well known for convenience, quality products, supporting local communities, and has over 4,400 stores in counties.

Our Mission and Vision

SPAR’s mission is to provide customers with high-quality products at affordable prices. We are committed to delivering excellent service and offering a wide range of products that meet the needs of every community we serve. While our vision is to be the most trusted and preferred retailer in every market we operate in. We aim to achieve this by supporting local businesses, promoting sustainability, and ensuring that their products and services improve the everyday lives of our customers.
